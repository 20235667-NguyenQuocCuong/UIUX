Design a modern mobile app called "UniMate" — a student productivity dashboard application.

Product Overview:
UniMate is a productivity app designed to help university students manage their academic life including schedules, deadlines, subjects, notes, and GPA tracking.

Target Users:
- University students aged 18–24
- Busy students managing multiple subjects
- Students who want to improve GPA and productivity

User Goals:
- Manage class schedule
- Track assignment deadlines
- Take subject notes
- Monitor GPA
- Stay organized

Pain Points:
- Forget deadlines
- Disorganized schedule
- Hard to track GPA
- Multiple tools scattered

Design Style:
- Clean modern UI
- Minimalist productivity dashboard
- Soft pastel colors
- iOS-style card UI
- Rounded corners (12–16px radius)
- Light mode design
- Soft shadows
- Spacious layout
